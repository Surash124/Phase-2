About Me

Who am I?

I’m Surash subba, a passionate Frontend Developer & UI/UX Designer with expertise in Python, JavaScript, and C++, dedicated to building responsive and user-friendly applications. Currently pursuing a Degree in Software Engineering from Collage of science and Technology, Bhutan.

Why I got interested in tech?

My journey into the world of technology began with a fascination for how things work and a desire to create. I was captivated by the endless possibilities of bringing ideas to life through code, and the satisfaction of building solutions that truly make a difference. This passion led me to pursue a career where I could constantly learn, innovate, and contribute to the ever-evolving digital landscape.

My design approach?

My design approach is rooted in user-centricity and functionality. I believe that powerful technology should be intuitive and aesthetically pleasing, creating seamless and engaging experiences. I strive to blend clean design with robust code, ensuring that every project is not only visually appealing but also highly performant and accessible. I'm a firm believer in iterative design, constantly refining and optimizing to achieve the best possible outcome.

What motivates me?

What truly motivates me is the challenge of solving complex problems and seeing the tangible impact of my work. I'm driven by a desire to craft elegant and efficient solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations. The opportunity to collaborate with diverse teams, learn new technologies, and contribute to projects that push boundaries is what inspires me to wake up every day. I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer and a designer.

Learning Journey

Over the past months, I've been exploring the fundamentals of computing and web design. Starting from simple Figma designs, I moved on to coding real web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Each project has helped me think more logically and solve problems more efficiently. I'm currently working on improving my understanding of how code and design come together to create smooth, responsive websites.

As a first-year software engineering student, I aim to strengthen my programming skills, build meaningful projects, and explore different areas of technology while continuously learning and growing as a developer.
